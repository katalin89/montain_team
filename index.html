Mountain Team Building

Winter, spring, summer or fall - there's a day of adventure on Grouse Mountain that will inspire your team. Our custom team-building events are designed to make a lasting impact. Best of all, we take your team goals and incorporate them into a fun-filled group experience! Mountain Team Building is the perfect break from the everyday. There are a variety of programs to choose from with several add-ons also available. Our adventures are suitable for groups of 10 to 400 people

Snowshoe fondue tour

Only at Grouse Mountain can you book an incredible snowshoe tour followed by a three-course fondue feast for your team-building adventure! After working up an appetite on a 1-hour mountain snowshoe tour, your group will enjoy scrumptious Cheese, Broth and Chocolate Fondue courses together in a beautiful room. This memorable experience lasts 3 hours in total and includes Mountain Admission, snowshoe rental, and a snowshoe guide.
